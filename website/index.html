Configure SMTP Recovery

Follow these simple steps to enable 2FA and password recovery features.

CRITICAL: If you do not configure SMTP details, your data cannot be retrieved if you forget your master password. This is your only recovery lifeline.

01

Open Settings

Launch the app and navigate to the Settings tab in the sidebar. This is where you manage your global configurations.

02

Enter Mail Details

Provide your Sender Email (the one sending OTPs) and your Receiver Email (where you receive them).

03

Secure App Password

Instead of your main password, use a 16-character Google App Password for the SMTP connection. This ensures your account remains protected.

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about SecureVault's security and privacy.

Is my data stored online?

No. SecureVault is 100% local. Your files and passwords never leave your computer, ensuring absolute privacy from hackers and third parties.

How does the recovery work?

We use SMTP (Simple Mail Transfer Protocol) to send a unique OTP to your registered email. This is the only way to reset your master password.

What is the 'Nuke' sequence?

The Nuke Sequence is an active defense tool. After 3 failed login attempts, the app can be configured to exfiltrate and purge sensitive vaults to prevent data theft.

Is it compatible with all drives?

Absolutely. SecureVault supports all NTFS and FAT32 volumes, including Internal HDDs/SSDs, External Hard Drives, and USB Flash Drives.
